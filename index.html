The Super Mario Bros Movie

A plumber called Mario travels to the Mushroom Kingdom to save his brother Luigi. While there, an evil dragon called Bowser takes over the kingdoms surrounding the Mushroom Kingdom.

I really liked this movie because this movie is inspired by one of my favourite video games of all time, Super Mario Bros. There are many references to the games throughout the years and I think the plot and humor is great!

  • Mario - First named Jumpman
  • Peach - Had her own DS game
  • Luigi - Is cowardly
  • Bowser - Mario's arch nemesis
